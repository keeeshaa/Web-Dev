Recipe #1: Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 3
Parmesan Cheese 50g

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.

Step 2: In a pan, fry the pancetta until crispy.

Step 3: Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Combine the spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture. Stir well and serve.

Recipe #2: Avocado Toast

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Avocado 1
Lemon Juice 1 tsp

Step 1: Toast the bread slices.

Step 2: Mash the avocado and mix with lemon juice.

Step 3: Spread the avocado mixture on the toast and serve.

Recipe #3: Chicken Onigiri

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cooked Rice 500g
Cooked Chicken 150g
Seaweed Sheets 4

Step 1: Season the cooked rice with a pinch of salt.

Step 2: Place a small amount of chicken in the center of a rice ball.

Step 3: Shape the rice into a triangle and wrap it with a seaweed sheet.

Step 4: Repeat for all portions and serve.
